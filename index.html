I'm a UX designer who graduated from UC San Diego. With a degree in Cognitive Science, I bring the interdisciplinary study of cognitive behavior and interactions as I rework problems into opportunities.s.

I believe design can have a big impact on our lives, especially in those that are often overlooked in our systems. Through data-driven decisions and attention to people's unique experiences, I pursue opportunities to design for social good no matter the challenge. It's in spaces such as Global Ties or the American Youth Literacy Foundation that I find my work the most satisfying.

When I'm not designing, you can find me dancing, drawing, playing DnD, and exploring nature! I'd love to hear from you as well! Feel free to chat with me at niki.tran72@gmail.com.
