Supporting Alzheimer Caregivers

Background

The impact of Alzheimer’s disease on the individual is devastating, and to witness as a family member is tragic. Not only do they have to transition into the role of a caregiver, but they also lose a sense of connection with their loved ones. The effect left on the caregiver is enormous. As a part of UCSD Design for America (DFA), my team wanted to focus on the needs of Alzheimer’s caregivers. DFA is nationally recognized human-centered design organization that uses design thinking to create a social impact, and we wanted to apply those design techniques to create a solution that would relieve the burdens that Alzheimer’s caregivers face.

Challenge
How can we relieve the burden caregivers of seniors
with Alzheimer's face?
