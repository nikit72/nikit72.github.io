NIKI TRAN

While navigating the many avenues in college, it wasn’t until I dove into design that I discovered it was the perfect way to incorporate the intersections of my passions. With a big interest in social work, people’s unique stories, and community building, user experience design allows me to work in a collaborative environment and bring visibility to even the most overlooked parts of people’s lives.

I believe design is a powerful tool that can impact people in subtle ways. Through UX design, I can explore that with the betterment of others in mind and create accessbile experiences for people often forgotten. As a designer, I aspire to create environments that empower and assist my users by celebrating their stories and experiences.

When I'm not designing, there's a lot of things that I like to get involved in as well! I dance and have led my own dance team, enjoy drawing by the beach, and get lost in nature while I camp. I also play Dungeons and Dragons with my friends and love finding ways to create community space. There's so much to learn out there!

Speaking of learning, I love learning and sharing stories! Feel free to chat with me via niki.tran72@gmail.com.
